Creation & Application of Forms

By: Anthony Miguel T. Ferrer

Form allows you to gather feedback from your users to give you data to improve your website.
They may leave suggestions and feedback through:

  • Textboxes
  • Password
  • Radio Boxes
  • Check Boxes
  • Dropdown Menu
  • Text Areas
  • Submit Button
  • Reset/Clear button
