Application of Hyperlinks

By: Anthony Miguel T. Ferrer

Hyperlinks are a link from a hypertext file or document to another location or file, typically activated by clicking on a highlighted word or image on the screen.
They use the anchor tag, or a. The anchor tag will not work without a destination. The destination is defined by the href, or hyperlink reference.

This is an example of a hyperlink

Click me to go to Youtube!

You can also use images as a hyperlink using the img tag.

Here is an example

Click on the YT logo
